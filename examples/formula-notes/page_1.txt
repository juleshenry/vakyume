Chapter 12: Advanced Pump Systems
12-1 Pump Efficiency
The efficiency of a pump is defined as the ratio of useful power to total power input.
eta = P_out / P_in
where:
eta := efficiency
P_out := useful power output
P_in := total power input

12-2 Compression Ratio
The compression ratio Cr is given by:
Cr = P_discharge / P_suction
where:
P_discharge := discharge pressure
P_suction := suction pressure
